NotificationHub Privacy Policy

by TeckGrow Consultancy

Privacy-First Approach: NotificationHub is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All your notification data stays on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Notification Data

NotificationHub accesses and processes the following information locally on your device:

  • Notification content (titles, messages, app sources)
  • Notification timestamps and read status
  • App package names and display names
  • Your app importance preferences and settings

1.2 App Settings and Preferences

  • Re-alert frequency settings
  • Data retention preferences
  • Silent notification preferences
  • Do Not Disturb behavior settings
  • App-specific importance classifications

2. How We Use Your Information

Local Processing Only: All data processing occurs exclusively on your device. We do not have access to your notification content or personal information.

Your information is used solely to:

  • Provide smart notification filtering and categorization
  • Generate re-alerts for important notifications you might miss
  • Maintain your app preferences and settings
  • Display notification history and analytics within the app
  • Optimize notification management based on your usage patterns

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

  • All data is stored locally on your device using encrypted databases
  • No cloud synchronization or external data transmission occurs
  • Data is automatically deleted based on your retention settings (default: 7 days)

3.2 Security Measures

  • Industry-standard local encryption for sensitive data
  • No network permissions for data transmission
  • Secure Android database implementation (Room)
  • Regular security updates and maintenance

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Zero Data Sharing: We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties, advertisers, or analytics services.
  • No analytics or tracking services are integrated
  • No user profiling or behavioral analysis for marketing purposes
  • No advertising networks have access to your data
  • No cloud services or external APIs receive your information

5. Your Privacy Rights and Controls

5.1 Data Control

  • Retention Control: Set how long notifications are stored (1-30 days)
  • App Selection: Choose which apps are monitored for important notifications
  • Complete Deletion: Clear all data at any time through app settings
  • Granular Permissions: Control notification access permissions

5.2 Data Export

You have the right to:

  • View all stored notification data within the app
  • Understand how your data is categorized and processed
  • Delete specific notifications or clear all data

6. Permissions and Access

6.1 Required Permissions

  • Notification Access: To read and categorize your notifications
  • Battery Optimization Exemption: To ensure reliable re-alerts
  • Boot Receiver: To restart monitoring after device reboot
  • Foreground Service: To provide timely re-alerts

6.2 Optional Permissions

  • Internet (Optional): Reserved for future features, currently unused

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Notifications are automatically deleted based on your retention settings
  • Default retention period is 7 days (configurable from 1-30 days)
  • App uninstallation completely removes all stored data
  • Manual data clearing is available in app settings

8. Children's Privacy

NotificationHub does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not target children specifically.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Users will be notified of significant changes through app updates.

10. Technical Implementation

10.1 Architecture

  • Built with modern Android architecture components
  • Uses Room database for efficient local storage
  • Implements Android notification listener best practices
  • Optimized for battery life and performance

10.2 Open Source Commitment

We believe in transparency. Key components of our privacy implementation:

  • Local-only data processing architecture
  • No network communication for user data
  • Encrypted local storage implementation
  • Regular security audits and updates
